Welcome!

In this HIT, you will play a fun drawing game. Your goal is to make drawings of various objects that you think will be recognizable to someone else.

A fun twist: sometimes you will be collaborating with a robot, taking turns producing one stroke each to make a recognizable drawing of some target object. Other times, you will make drawings on your own.

Please only draw the object and do NOT draw any surrounding context (e.g., water, grass). Please do NOT draw any words or non-pictorial symbols (e.g., arrows).

We expect the average game to last approximately 15-20 minutes.

Note: We strongly recommend using Chrome. Please avoid resizing or refreshing the browser window during this HIT. If you have any questions or run into any issues, please send us an email (sketchloop@gmail.com ). Please pay attention and do your best! Thank you!
